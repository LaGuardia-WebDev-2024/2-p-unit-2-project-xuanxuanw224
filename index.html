“Found Feathers”, a seemingly lighthearted and innocent film that takes on a dark twist when faced with the realities of deforestation. Owle, an owl unique from any other lived a peaceful life far from the brutalities of nature. He chooses to eat an omnivorous diet to avoid harming other lives, and collects unique leafs to fancy as head accessories. However when a leaf he treasured went missing, he was determined to get it back.
The film is intended to raise awareness for deforestation. It hurts hundreds of thousands of species by destroying their habitats, food chains, and basically every part of their world. Help by learning more, supporting forest conservation, spreading the message, and even going out and planting a tree. A little goes a long way.
